US Birth Data Dashboard

The dashboard reflects U.S. births data for the years 2000 to 2014, as provided by the Social Security Administration.

How to use

Simply select your dates by choosing relevant bars, and find out the total number of births for the selected time frame.

To reset, click the baby icon located at the top left
